5 Ingredient Chili

Description

A fast, simple, and delecious 5-ingredient chili recipe that everyone can enjoy.

Made from ground beef, tomato sauce, kidney beans, chili beans, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Easy as that. I guess that's really 6 ingredients, but whose counting...

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 15 ounces of tomato sauce
  • 1 (15oz) can of kidney beans, drained
  • 1 (15oz) can of chili beans, not drained
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. Stir in kidney beans, chili beans, and tomato sauce. Bring to a boil and stir in chili powder. Reduce heat to a simmer, and cook, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 15 minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
